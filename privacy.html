Privacy Policy — Yuvi.AI Instagram Automation

Last updated: June 2026

This application ("the App") is operated by Yuvi.AI for the Instagram professional account @yuvi.ai1. This page explains what data the App processes and why.

What the App does

When a user comments on one of our Instagram posts, the App may post a public reply under that comment and send the user a one-time private message (direct message) containing a link to free content they requested (for example, a guide).

Data we process

  • The comment ID and comment text;
  • The commenter's public Instagram user ID and username;
  • The media (post) ID the comment was made on.

This data is used solely to deliver the public reply and the private message. We do not sell or share it with third parties, and we do not build user profiles.

Data retention & deletion

We temporarily store a comment identifier (up to 7 days) only to avoid sending duplicate replies. We do not retain personal data beyond what is needed to operate the automation. To request deletion of any data or to ask a question, contact us at the email below.

Messaging policy

The App only replies privately to users who initiated contact by commenting on our content. It does not send unsolicited or promotional messages, and it sends at most one private reply per comment.
